🚲 Burlington Bike Route Planner

Burlington is a great biking city, but it can be hard to find new routes that feel safe and low-traffic. This map shows bike paths, quiet residential streets, and gravel paths so you can plan rides and commutes without ending up on busy roads.

How to use it

🗺️
Explore the map Roads are color-coded by type. Toggle tiers on/off using the filter buttons at the top.
✏️
Draw a route Click "Draw route," then click along the map to add waypoints. Routes snap to roads automatically. Double-click to finish.
💾
Save & export Name your route and save it. View the elevation profile, or export as GPX to load into Strava or Ride with GPS.
👁️
Manage routes Toggle routes on/off or reverse their direction from the saved routes panel.
